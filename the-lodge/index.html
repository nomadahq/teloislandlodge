Activities

Fishing

Head out for a fishing mission on a slow afternoon, cold beverage in hand. Our guides and staff are enthusiastic anglers who'll take care of the messy side of things for you.

If you haven't feasted on a Mahi Mahi you caught only hours earlier while being complimented by your fellow surfers at dinnertime, then you haven't experienced true happiness.

Activities

Snorkelling

Picture this. You're anchored at the E.R. on a glassy small day. You've had a dozen waves under your belt and are waiting for the crew to finish.

You grab a snorkel set (always available), roll overboard, and go lose yourself in a tropical aquarium for half an hour. The visibility, the robust reef ecosystem, and a roster of curious fish darting around everywhere. Sublime stuff.

SUP veteran or not, by the end of your trip you'll be proudly paddling the lagoon in full view of your comrades, exclaiming over the full-body workout benefits.

Culture

Telo Village Bike Tour

A time-honoured tradition among Telo Lodge regulars. Know what it's like when a bad-ass motorcycle gang rolls into town? The rumble? The leather? The ambivalent handlebar moustaches?

Well, this is nothing like that. Picture a few dorky middle-aged westerners (that's you) on small hire motorbikes and scooters, in loose formation, laughing, smiling, and waving g'day to the locals ‚Äî who are laughing and smiling right back.

A fun way to interact with the nearby village, get a taste of the local culture, and get any mid-life desire to own a Harley Davidson out of your system.
