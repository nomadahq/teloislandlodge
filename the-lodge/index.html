Dining

The Kitchen

The food at Telo Island Lodge is spectacular. All-inclusive meals feature fresh, healthy nourishment — seafood pulled from the ocean that morning, tropical fruits, and dishes that fuse Indonesian tradition with western favourites.

Meals vary between buffet and table service. Dietary requirements accommodated with advance notice. And let's not get started on dessert.

Recovery

Massage & Restoration

Our masseuse could write a book on coaxing surfers' arms, shoulders and spines back into working order. Whether a long-time massage lover or newcomer, you'll want to plan on including this as part of your trip.

Adventure

Fishing

Head out for a fishin' mission on a slow afternoon, cold beverage in hand. Our guides and staff are enthusiastic anglers who'll take care of the messy side of things for you.

If you haven't feasted on a Mahi Mahi you caught only hours earlier while being complimented by fellow appreciative surfers at dinnertime, then you haven't experienced true happiness.

On the Water

Stand Up Paddle & Snorkelling

SUP veteran or no, by the end of your trip you'll be proudly paddling the lagoon in full view of your comrades. The E.R. reef offers world-class snorkelling — robust ecosystem, curious fish darting everywhere. Sublime stuff.

Culture

Telo Village Bike Tour

A time-honoured tradition among regulars. Picture a few dorky middle-aged westerners on small hire scooters, in loose formation, laughing, smiling, and waving g'day to the locals — who are laughing and smiling right back.

A fun way to interact with the nearby village, get a taste of local culture, and get any mid-life desire to own a Harley out of your system.
