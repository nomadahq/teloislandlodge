Performance Shortboard

El Patron

Matt Biolos' personal board — a contemporary performance shortboard scaled up for properly-proportioned adults. Low entry rocker, accelerated tail rocker, shallow single concave, slight vee through the fins, flat out the tail. Hidden volume under the chest for a performance board with age-friendly dimensions.

Step-Up

Crowd Killer

When the swell picks up and you need something with more paddle power and hold. The Crowd Killer bridges the gap between your everyday shortboard and a full step-up gun. Extra length and volume where you need it, refined rail for control in bigger surf.

Small Wave Performance

Puddle Jumper HP

For those mellower days when the swell drops. Wide point forward for easy entry, performance rocker keeps it lively on the wave face. Extra volume without the sluggishness — surprisingly responsive for a board this user-friendly.

All-Rounder

RNF Redux

The Swiss Army knife. Round nose fish outline with modern rocker and fin placement. Covers the widest range of conditions — from knee-high fun waves to solid overhead days. If you're only renting one board, this is probably the one.

Price is based on a full-trip rental. A $400 USD security deposit is also required. All boards are C4 construction — lighter, stronger, and more responsive than standard PU/PE. Surfboard rentals must be booked on a full-trip basis and are subject to availability.
