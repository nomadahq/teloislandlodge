Your Guides

We Know These Waves

Dingo and the crew have surfed these breaks for over a decade. They read the swell, the wind, the tide — and match it to your ability. Whether it's your first reef break or your hundredth barrel, they'll put you in the right spot.

Two speedboats run simultaneously so you're never stuck at the wrong break. And when the conditions change mid-session? They'll have the other boat waiting.
