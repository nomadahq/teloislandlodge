The Surf

Twenty Breaks. Zero Crowds.

Perfect Sumatran reef surf, less intense than the Mentawais but more groomed. Over 20 options all within a 25-minute speedboat ride. Shortboarding, longboarding, or anywhere in between.

The Ultimate Surf Playground

Telo Island Lodge has two world-class waves on its doorstep and eighteen more accessible in less than half an hour. Even by Indonesian surf standards, that's special.

Our Marine Ops team are on the case from dawn and will find the right break in the right conditions to perfectly suit your abilities. Every session, every day.

Surf Breaks

The Lineup

Our guide team knows every reef, every tide, every wind angle. Here are some of the highlights.

Right Reef All Levels
Max's Right

The wave we wake up to each morning. The wave we love. A freight-train right breaking directly in front of the lodge — visible from your room, the bar, the deck. Head-high and fun most days, overhead and pumping when the Indian Ocean delivers. No boat required.

Right Reef Advanced
GT's

Intense right-hand barrel similar to HT's in the Mentawais — but without the surgeon's table waiting at the end. Fast, hollow, and rewarding for experienced surfers who want to test themselves.

Left Reef Intermediate+
Le Ba

What can we say? The photo says it all. One of the most photogenic waves in the Telos — a long, wrapping left that offers barrels, walls, and endless opportunity. Let's go.

Right Reef Intermediate
The Bubble

Spirited wrapping little righthander. The perfect blend of intensity and user-friendliness. Fast, fun, and the kind of wave you'll talk about at the bar later. A Telo favourite.

Right Reef All Levels
The E.R.

Trusted swell magnet. Head-high fun when the Indian Ocean is docile elsewhere. The reef pass here also doubles as an incredible snorkelling spot — roll overboard between sets and lose yourself in a tropical aquarium.

Left Reef Intermediate
Churches

A long, peeling left that lets you set your rail and draw lines all the way down the reef. Consistent, predictable, and deeply satisfying. One of those waves you never want to end.

Right Reef All Levels
Pinnacles

Horseshoe wall made for cruising and big ol' rail turns. Astonishingly beautiful postcard backdrop. Picture-perfect peaky wave, surfable from waist-high up to head-high. One of the most scenic spots you'll ever surf.

Right Reef Intermediate+
Kindy's

High-performance peak just down from the Schoolyards setup. Custom-made for throwing gratifying fans of spray out of the lip. When it's on, this is a world-class rippable right.

Left Reef Intermediate
Schoolyards

Expansive reef with several distinct takeoff sections. Swell magnet with walls that grow before your eyes as you race down the line. Multiple peaks mean there's always a wave with your name on it.
