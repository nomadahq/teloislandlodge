Location

Where in the World

Situated off the west coast of Sumatra, almost directly on the equator, Telo Island Lodge is ensconced in an archipelago known as "Kepulan Batu", "Pulau Pulau Batu" or, more simply, the Telo Islands.

We are in good company, with the Mentawai Islands to the south and Nias to the north, yet the islands in our group are unlike any in Sumatra — often compared to the dramatic cliff islands of Thailand or the smaller islands of the Seychelles.

Due to the remote location — and the fact it can only be accessed conveniently by private aircraft — the Telos remain largely unspoiled by the development seen in many parts of Indonesia.

Our Story

Fifteen Years in Remote Indonesia

Remote area construction and operation has been part of our lives since the beginning, including our first project — Hinako's Hideaway in the Hinako Islands — which ran for 15 years.

Telo Island Lodge is the original Telo surf outpost and has been going strong for well over a decade. We trailblazed the logistical challenges of the area and have been refining our approach constantly.

The lodge continues to evolve — with the addition of a dedicated lounge and games bungalow, expanded room options, a professional guide team, and partnerships with brands like …Lost Surfboards. What hasn't changed is the core promise: uncrowded world-class surf, genuine hospitality, and an experience you won't find anywhere else.

Local Culture

The Batu Islanders

This is a culture strikingly different to western society. Batu Islanders have a distinct, vibrant culture with a mix of Christian, Islamic, and Animist beliefs. Life here — centred around the village — plays out in a sustenance economy based primarily on fishing and coconut farming.

Batu Islanders are some of the most friendly, happy, and family-oriented folks you'll ever come across.

We strive to be a positive force in the local Indonesian communities. We purchase local building materials and food, favour and employ local labour, and have a long history of involvement with local education projects.

Community

Giving Back

We believe in being part of the communities we operate in — not just alongside them. 100% of guest donations go directly to local village projects including education, infrastructure, and community development.

Please join us in helping our local villages. Every bit makes a difference.
