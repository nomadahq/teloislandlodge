The Lodge

Where the Jungle Meets the Indian Ocean

Situated lagoon-side at the base of a small mountain, the lodge commands a vista over one of Indonesia's most coveted surfing locations. Open-plan post-and-beam construction fuses local North Sumatran style with western comfort.

The bar and lounge is the epicentre of good vibes — coffees in the morning, post-surf recovery after lunch, beers and hilarity in the evening. All-inclusive meals feature seafood pulled from the ocean that morning, tropical fruits, and dishes that fuse Indonesian tradition with western favourites.

Explore the Lodge
Your Wave

Max's Right

The lodge's signature wave. A freight-train right breaking directly in front — visible from your room, the bar, the deck. Head-high and fun most days, overhead and pumping when the Indian Ocean delivers.

No boat required. Roll out of bed, paddle out, score. And when you want more? Twenty additional breaks — lefts, rights, barrels, cruisers — all within a 25-minute speedboat ride.
