The Lodge

Where the Jungle Meets the Indian Ocean

Situated lagoon-side at the base of a small mountain, Telo Island Lodge commands a vista over one of Indonesia's most coveted surfing locations. The lodge is an open-plan post-and-beam construction fusing local North Sumatran style with western comfort.

The bar and lounge is the epicentre of good vibes — coffees in the morning, post-surf recovery after lunch, beers and hilarity in the evening. Spacious covered decks and manicured lawns surround a tropical lagoon where our speedboat fleet stands ready.

All-inclusive meals prepared by our kitchen team deliver fresh, healthy nourishment — seafood pulled from the ocean that morning, tropical fruits, and dishes that fuse Indonesian tradition with western favourites. Dietary requirements accommodated with advance notice.

Overwater Bungalow · 2 Rooms

Upper & Lower Bungalow

The legendary overwater bungalow — two rooms stacked above the reef. Wake to the ocean beneath you. Upper floor catches the breeze, lower floor is steps from the water. Ensuite, A/C, king beds.

Lodge Room · 2 Rooms

Beachfront Lodge Rooms

In the main lodge building, steps from the ocean. No direct water view but you're in the heart of everything — the kitchen, the bar, the deck, and Max's Right breaking out front. Ensuite, A/C, king beds.

Waterfront Triple · 1 Room

The Triple

Right on the water, our largest room sleeps three comfortably. Perfect for friends travelling together or families. Ensuite, A/C, and front-row seats to the Indian Ocean.

Your Wave

Max's Right

TIL's signature wave. A freight-train right breaking directly in front of the lodge — visible from your room, the bar, the deck. Head-high and fun most days, overhead and pumping when the Indian Ocean delivers.

No boat required. Roll out of bed, paddle out, score.

And when you want more? Twenty additional breaks — lefts, rights, barrels, cruisers — all within a 25-minute speedboat ride. Our guides know exactly where to take you based on the swell, the tide, and your ability.
